Our Story

Cupid in the Cloud was founded in 2025 with a dream to connect hearts in a fast-paced digital world. From a small team of visionaries, we've grown into a beloved platform that celebrates love in all forms. Our community is built on trust, honesty, and the belief that everyone deserves a chance at real love.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to go beyond superficial connections and create a genuine, emotionally, intelligent space where people find love through mutual understanding and compatibility. We focus on emotional matchmaking, meaningful engagement, and digital safety, ensuring every interaction is built on honesty and care.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology enhance emotional connections, not replaces it. Cupid in the Cloud is more than just a dating site-it's a digital sanctuary where hearts meet, grow, and thrive. We aim to build a future where love is accessible, inclusive, and celebrated in all its forms-across borders, beliefs, and barriers.
